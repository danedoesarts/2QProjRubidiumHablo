Project Management

by Dane Hablo | Life Skills

When working with big projects, from creating a 2D animated short film to developing a product for Science and Technology, you may be coordinating with different people on a number of different tasks, all with different deadlines. But even if you're working on these alone, it can be overwhelming to manage so many spinning plates. Mismanaging them can lead to burnout or delays producing something with less of the expected quality or even the total cancellation of the project.
